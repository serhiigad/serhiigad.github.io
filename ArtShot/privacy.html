Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 17, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how ArtShot ("we", "our", or "the Game") handles your information. By playing ArtShot, you agree to the terms of this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal identification information (such as your name or address) directly. However, we may collect:

  • Email Address: If you sign in via Google, we use your email to identify your profile in our database (Supabase) and save your game progress.
  • Device Identifiers: We may use device tokens for advertising (AdMob) to show you relevant ads and for basic analytics.

2. Third-Party Services

The Game uses the following third-party services that may collect information used to identify you:

  • Google Play Services
  • AdMob
  • Supabase (Database)

3. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information and game progress. Data is accessed only for game-related purposes.

4. Account and Data Deletion

We provide a way for users to request the deletion of their account and associated data. If you wish to delete your game profile and all related data (including your email address and game progress):

  • You can send a deletion request to our support email provided in the Google Play Store.
  • Upon request, we will delete your record from our Supabase database within 7 days.
  • Please note that some data may be retained if required by law or for legitimate business purposes (e.g., fraud prevention).

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or data deletion, you can contact us via the contact information provided in the Google Play Store.
